VISITOR GUIDE

Eco Park Rules & Guidelines

Stick to the walking path

Remaining on the designated walking path is essential to preserving the park’s natural habitats. Straying off the path can harm plants and disrupt wildlife.

Supervise children & pets

Ensure children and pets are closely supervised for their safety and to prevent accidents or disturbances within the park.

Be respectful to other visitors

Maintain a peaceful environment by keeping noise low, sharing the walking path, and being mindful of others’ enjoyment.

Don’t pick flowers or plants

Avoid picking plants or flowers as it negatively affects the ecosystem and the park’s natural beauty.

Dispose of trash properly

Help keep the park clean by using proper trash and recycling bins. Responsible disposal protects wildlife and maintains the park environment.
