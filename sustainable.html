Sustainable Practices

Reduce plastic use

To reduce plastic, avoid single-use items like straws and bags by carrying reusable alternatives, buy products in bulk to minimize packaging, and choose products with minimal or plastic-free packaging.

Recycle when possible

Recycling is beneficial for the environment, and you should recycle by separating common recyclables like paper, cardboard, glass, and certain plastics.

Carry reusable water bottles

Using a reusable water bottle saves money, reduces plastic waste, and helps you stay hydrated. You can refill it after passing airport security when traveling.

Participate in eco-friendly actions

Picking up litter is a simple but impactful action that reduces pollution, protects wildlife, and builds community pride. It can be done individually or in groups.
